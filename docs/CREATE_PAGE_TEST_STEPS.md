# Create Page Lean Layout – Manual Test Steps

## 10 Manual Test Steps

1. **Generate from describe** – Enter a prompt in Describe mode (e.g. "Cozy watercolor portrait of a cat") and click "Generate design". Confirm the design is generated and you move to step 2.

2. **Upload mode shows upload only** – Click the "Upload a photo" tab. Confirm the upload area is visible (dashed border, choose photo). Switch back to Describe; confirm the upload area is hidden.

3. **Prompt helper fills main prompt** – Expand "Need help writing it?", pick Who/Style/Mood/Background, click "Use this prompt". Confirm the main prompt input is filled with the built prompt.

4. **Region switch changes "Ideas for you"** – Change region (e.g. US to UK) via the region selector. Confirm "Ideas for you" shows different holiday badges and suggested prompts.

5. **Prompt chip fills input (replace confirm works)** – With an existing prompt, click a suggested prompt chip. Confirm the inline "Replace current prompt?" appears with Replace/Cancel. Click Replace and confirm the prompt updates. Repeat with Cancel to confirm it clears.

6. **Product picker still works** – Click a product (T-Shirts, Mugs, Cards, Hoodies) before or after generating. Confirm you move to step 2 with the selected product.

7. **Placement still correct** – Generate a design, pick a product and color. Confirm the mockup shows the design placed correctly on the product. Change colors; confirm placement remains accurate.

8. **AI assistant unchanged** – Open the AI gift assistant (bottom-right). Confirm its UI, text, and behavior are unchanged from before.

9. **Testimonials show once** – Scroll the create page (step 1). Confirm there is a single "What our creators say" carousel with testimonials, and no duplicate testimonial sections.

10. **Checkout still works** – Add a design to cart or proceed to checkout. Click "Pay £X" and complete the Stripe flow (or use a test card). Confirm redirect to success page.
